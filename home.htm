11.24.24
Wedding Day

The wedding day is divided into four parts. It is all in one venue (Marquis Events Place, BGC).

  • 3:00 PM - 3:45 PM

    The Pre-ceremony Gathering

    Please come and catch up with Jorge and his family at the venue. There will be some food and booths open for you to enjoy during this time.

  • 4:00 PM - 4:45 PM

    Ceremony & Worship

    Food and booths will close down for this important moment where the two become one.

  • 4:45 PM - 6:30 PM

    Activities and mini-events

    This is a 2 hour long break between programs where you can enjoy a lot of activities and mini-events. Delicious food and drinks are available. We have massage booths, photobooths, live watercolor artists, arcades, game booths, etc. that you can enjoy. You can record a message at our audio guestbook. There will also be a mini-event involving the bachelors and bachelorettes in the party (all who have happily consented prior!).

  • 6:30 PM onwards

    Reception

    Enjoy the rest of the night at the reception with our star Chef Stephan of Metiz Restaurant (Asia’s Top 50) along with our fun and meaningful program. Watch our anime video created by CoreForce Works, listen to speeches, play a few games, and celebrate this special evening with us.

    IMPORTANT: This will be a plated dinner so let us know at the RSVP section if you can not come.
